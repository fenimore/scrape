#Legend:
The larger the marker (circle) on the free-stuff map, the more recent the posting, 
At the present there are two stuff-distinctions: BLUE are electronics (tv, speakers, etc) and RED are furniture (chairs, wood, 
etc). Circles colored BLACK are the "desired" stuffs (here books or "cool" things). The default color is WHITE.
